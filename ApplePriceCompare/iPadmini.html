iPad mini 5

Available in Silver, Space gray, or Gold, the iPad mini 5 is 5.3-inches by 8.0-inches and 0.24-inches thick. It has a standard storage capacity of 64GB that can be upgraded to 256GB for users who need more storage space. The 7.9-inch fingerprint-resistant display features a 2048-by-1536 resolution at 326 pixels per inch, supports the P3 color gamut, and has True Tone technology, which shifts colors to account for ambient lighting. The iPad mini 5 has an 8MP rear camera with a five-element lens that is capable of taking panoramic, live and HDR photos. It can capture 1080p HD video, slo-mo video at 240 frames-per-second, and time lapse video. The front-facing camera is a 7MP FaceTime HD camera. Touch ID makes unlocking your iPad a snap, and provides security when making purchases in the App Store. The 2019 iPad mini is also the first iPad mini to support the Apple Pencil. This iPad comes bundled with a Lightning-to-USB cable and a USB power adapter.
